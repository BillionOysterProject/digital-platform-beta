Learn and practice restoration ecology in New York Harbor by sharing data, curriculum, research, and more through the BOP-CCERS Digital Platform!

How it works

Every school gets their own waterfront Oyster Research Station to collect and publish data. Students conduct their own field research projects using the digital platform, then present to professional scientists and policy makers at the Annual BOP Symposium. Teachers use the platform to collaborate on BOP curriculum, share resources, register for events, and more. The Billion Oyster Project is a curriculum and community enterprise for restoration science. Students are at the center. Help them learn to restore, restore to learn!
