Species ID Resources

The BOP Digital Platform's database contains information about the mobile and sessile organisms most commonly found in Oyster Research Stations. Each listing includes a link to the species' profile on iNaturalist, which includes multiple photos of the organism and a worldwide map of sightings by community scientists, along with more detailed information about the species.
