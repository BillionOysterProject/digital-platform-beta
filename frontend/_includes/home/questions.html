About Billion Oyster Project (BOP)

Billion Oyster Project's mission is to restore oyster reefs to New York Harbor through public education initiatives. Our vision is a future in which New York Harbor is the center of a rich, diverse, and abundant estuary. The communities that surround this complex ecosystem have helped construct it, and in return benefit from it, with endless opportunities for work, education, and recreation.
