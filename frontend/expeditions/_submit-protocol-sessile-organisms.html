Sessile Organism Identifications

Your ORS can have up to four settlement tiles on it. Each column below is used to enter data for one settlement tile. Enter each sessile organism species you identified at the center of each cross hatch in the table below.

Start typing either the commmon or scientific name of the sessile organism, then select it from the dropdown menu.

Tip: After you select an organism name, hit enter, then move your cursor to the bottom right corner of the cell until a "+" appears. You can click the + and drag it down or to the left or right to repeat the organism name.

For help identifying sessile organisms, check out our Sessile Organisms page.
