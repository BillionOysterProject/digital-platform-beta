Report a wild oyster sighting

We're currently working on an Oyster Recruitment protocol. This will give us information about oyster reproduction and survival of juvenile oysters at BOP restoration sites. The presence of oyster recruits (i.e., “spat”) may indicate reproducing wild populations nearby or the successful reproduction of restored oysters.

For now, if you think you see a wild oyster, please report it to us! Email bop.digital.platform@nyharbor.org with close-up photos and our scientists will take a look!
